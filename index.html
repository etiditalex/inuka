About Inuka Afrika Properties Limited

IAPL is a legally registered limited liability company with its head office located in Nyali, Mombasa. Our core business is in real estate, mainly land and affordable housing, majorly focused in coastal Kenya and in 2019 moved to Nairobi area with properties and projects in the outskirts of Nairobi.

We offer a complete package of affordable real estate investment solutions, ensuring we meet customers' property and land ownership from sales and rentals, retail and commercial to mortgage, new development marketing, property management and title issuance.
