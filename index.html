About Inuka Afrika Properties Limited

IAPL is a legally registered limited liability company with its head office located in Nyali, Mombasa. Our core business is in real estate, including residential properties, commercial properties, beach properties, farm land in Chakama, and affordable housing offplan projects, focused in Kilifi County with properties in Mariakani, Mtwapa, Kikambala, Kilifi, Bofa, Tezo, Chumani, Msabaha, and Malindi.

We offer a complete package of affordable real estate investment solutions, ensuring we meet customers' property and land ownership from sales and rentals, retail and commercial to mortgage, new development marketing, property management and title issuance.
