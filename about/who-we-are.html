About Inuka Afrika Properties Limited

Inuka Afrika Properties Limited (IAPL) is a legally registered limited liability company with its head office located in Nyali, Mombasa. Founded with a vision to transform the real estate landscape in Kenya, IAPL has grown from a coastal-focused company to a national leader in affordable real estate solutions.

Our core business is in real estate, mainly land and affordable housing, majorly focused in coastal Kenya. In 2019, we expanded our operations to the Nairobi area with properties and projects in the outskirts of Nairobi, demonstrating our commitment to serving clients across the country.

We offer a complete package of affordable real estate investment solutions, ensuring we meet customers' property and land ownership needs from sales and rentals, retail and commercial to mortgage, new development marketing, property management and title issuance.

Our Organization Structure

Inuka Afrika Properties Limited has created employment opportunities for over a hundred people in various positions in the organization with two branch offices in Nairobi and Mombasa as the head office.

The company's organization structure is managed by a Board of Directors (BOD) comprising of three directors who are founders of the company. The General Manager (GM) who directly reports to the BOD and manages the daily activities of the company.

The senior management team consists of the Sales and Marketing Manager, Finance Manager, Project Manager and Compliance Manager, as head of respective departments and they report directly to the General Manager of the company.
