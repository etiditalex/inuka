Property Description

Mida Park View Phase 2 is strategically located in Mida, just before Mida Msikitini stage on the right hand side towards Malindi. This affordable development offers excellent accessibility and security for discerning buyers seeking value in Kilifi County.

The project is approximately 800 meters from the Kilifi-Malindi highway, providing easy access to major transportation routes. A 9-meter wide, compact murram road ensures smooth access to the site, while a perimeter fence has been constructed around the project for added security.
