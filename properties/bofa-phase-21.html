Property Description

Bofa Phase 21 offers premium development plots located off the newly tarmacked Bofa Road (B69), providing excellent accessibility and modern infrastructure. This development combines the convenience of urban living with the tranquility of a well-planned residential area, making it perfect for both residential development and investment opportunities.
