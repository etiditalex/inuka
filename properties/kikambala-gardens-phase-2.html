Property Description

Kikambala Gardens Phase 2 is a serene coastal development located opposite the Sultan Palace junction, just 2.5km from the highway. The project sits on the main murram access road and offers reliable access to utilities and key social amenities.

With a secure perimeter fence, 9m wide access roads, and a peaceful environment with indigenous trees like mango and palm, this project is ideal for residential development, holiday homes, and investment opportunities.
