Bofa Platinum Estate Launch Event: A Vision for the Future

Inuka Afrika Properties invites investors and vendors to celebrate the grand launch of our proposed Bofa Platinum Estate development on September 27, 2025.

Conceptual rendering of the proposed Bofa Platinum Estate development in Kilifi County.

Inuka Afrika Properties Limited is thrilled to announce the grand launch event for our proposed Bofa Platinum Estate development, scheduled for September 27, 2025. This landmark event, set to take place in the vibrant Kilifi County, will showcase our vision for a premium residential development and mark a significant milestone in our company's growth trajectory.

A Celebration of Vision and Opportunity

The Bofa Platinum Estate launch event is more than just a presentation; it's a celebration of our vision for the future of residential living in Kilifi County. We extend a warm invitation to all potential investors, partners, and vendors to join us in exploring this exciting development opportunity.

This event will serve as a unique platform for various vendors to showcase their products and services, fostering local economic growth and providing attendees with a diverse range of offerings. From construction materials to home improvement solutions, the event promises to be a hub of activity and opportunity.

Why Bofa Platinum Estate is a Premier Investment Opportunity

Bofa Platinum Estate represents our vision for a premium residential development that will set new standards in Kilifi County. Key features that make this proposed development exceptional include:

  • Prime location in the rapidly developing Kilifi County
  • Planned infrastructure with access roads, water, and electricity
  • Proximity to essential amenities like schools, hospitals, and shopping centers
  • Secure and serene environment, ideal for residential development
  • Clear title deeds and transparent acquisition process
  • Modern architectural designs and sustainable building practices
  • Flexible payment plans to make ownership accessible
