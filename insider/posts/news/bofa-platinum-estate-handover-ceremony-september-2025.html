Bofa Platinum Estate Handover Ceremony - September 27, 2025

A Grand Milestone Event Celebrating the Completion of Our Premium Development in Kilifi County

Bofa Platinum Estate - A stunning aerial view showcasing the completed development with modern infrastructure and beautiful landscaping

We are thrilled to announce a momentous occasion in our company's history - the official handover ceremony of Bofa Platinum Estate to our valued clients on September 27, 2025. This grand event will be hosted in the heart of Kilifi County, marking the successful completion of one of our most prestigious development projects.

A Celebration of Excellence and Partnership

The Bofa Platinum Estate handover ceremony represents more than just the completion of a real estate project; it symbolizes the culmination of years of planning, dedication, and partnership between Inuka Afrika Properties and our esteemed investors. This premium development has set new standards for luxury living in Kilifi County, featuring state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern amenities, and sustainable design principles.

Event Details and Invitations

Date & Time

September 27, 2025
10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Venue

Bofa Platinum Estate
Kilifi County, Kenya

Expected Guests

500+ Investors, Vendors & Dignitaries

Who's Invited to This Grand Event

🎯 Primary Investors

All investors who have participated in the Bofa Platinum Estate project are cordially invited to witness the handover of their investment. This is your moment to see the tangible results of your trust in our vision and to receive the keys to your premium property investment.

🏪 Vendor Showcase Opportunity

We are excited to invite vendors from various industries to showcase their products and services during this grand event. This presents an excellent opportunity to:

  • Connect with property owners and potential clients
  • Demonstrate your products and services to a targeted audience
  • Network with other vendors and industry professionals
  • Gain exposure in the growing Kilifi County market
  • Establish partnerships with property management companies
