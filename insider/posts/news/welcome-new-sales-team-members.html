Welcome to Our New Sales Team Members

Five experienced professionals join our growing team to better serve our clients

Our expanded sales team is ready to provide exceptional service to our valued clients

We're excited to welcome five new sales professionals to our growing team. These experienced professionals will help us better serve our clients and expand our market reach across Kenya's most promising real estate locations.

Meet Our New Team Members

Our new sales team members bring a wealth of experience and expertise to Inuka Properties. Each team member has been carefully selected for their proven track record in real estate sales, customer service excellence, and deep understanding of the Kenyan property market.

Senior Sales Consultant

10+ years experience in luxury real estate sales across Kenya

Investment Specialist

Expert in commercial and residential investment properties

Client Relations Manager

Dedicated to ensuring exceptional customer experience

Location Specialist

Deep knowledge of Kilifi County and coastal properties

What This Means for Our Clients

The addition of these experienced professionals means our clients will benefit from:

  • More personalized attention and dedicated support
  • Faster response times to inquiries and requests
  • Expert guidance on property investment decisions
  • Expanded market knowledge and property insights
  • Enhanced customer service across all touchpoints

Our Commitment to Excellence

Each new team member has undergone comprehensive training on our company values, processes, and commitment to client satisfaction. They understand that at Inuka Properties, we don't just sell properties – we help our clients make informed investment decisions that align with their long-term goals.

Our new team members are equipped with the latest tools and technologies to provide seamless service, from initial property viewing to final transaction completion. They are committed to maintaining the high standards of professionalism and integrity that our clients have come to expect from Inuka Properties.

Expanding Our Reach

With our expanded team, we can now better serve clients across different regions and property types. Our new team members bring specialized knowledge in various aspects of real estate, allowing us to provide more comprehensive services to our diverse client base.

Whether you're looking for residential properties, commercial investments, or land for development, our expanded team has the expertise to guide you through every step of the process.

Get to Know Our Team

We encourage our clients to reach out and get to know our new team members. They are here to help you find the perfect property investment opportunity and ensure your experience with Inuka Properties exceeds your expectations.

You can meet our new team members by visiting our office, scheduling a property viewing, or simply calling us to discuss your real estate needs. We're confident you'll find them knowledgeable, professional, and committed to your success.
