New Office Opening in Kilifi Town

Strategic location to better serve our growing client base in Kilifi County

Our new office in Kilifi Town brings us closer to our clients and properties

We're pleased to announce the opening of our new office in Kilifi Town. This strategic location will better serve our growing client base in Kilifi County and provide easier access to our properties and services for local residents and investors.

Strategic Location Benefits

Our new Kilifi Town office is strategically positioned to serve our expanding client base in the region. Located in the heart of Kilifi Town, the office provides convenient access for clients interested in our properties across Kilifi County.

Central Location

Easy access from all parts of Kilifi County and surrounding areas

Extended Hours

Convenient operating hours to accommodate client schedules

Local Team

Dedicated local staff with deep knowledge of the area

Property Access

Quick access to all our Kilifi County properties for site visits

Services Available at the New Office

Our Kilifi Town office offers the full range of Inuka Properties services, including:

  • Property consultations and investment advice
  • Site visits and property viewing arrangements
  • Documentation and transaction processing
  • Customer service and support
  • Information about upcoming projects and developments
  • Financing assistance and partnership referrals

Office Details and Contact Information

Our new Kilifi Town office is fully equipped with modern facilities and a professional team ready to serve our clients. The office features comfortable consultation areas, property display materials, and all necessary technology to provide seamless service.

What This Means for Our Clients

The opening of our Kilifi Town office represents our commitment to providing exceptional service to our clients in the region. With a local presence, we can offer more personalized service, faster response times, and better understanding of local market conditions.

Clients in Kilifi County and surrounding areas can now enjoy the convenience of visiting our office for consultations, property viewings, and transaction processing without having to travel to our main office in Mombasa.

Grand Opening Celebration

We invite all our clients, partners, and community members to join us for the grand opening celebration of our new Kilifi Town office. The event will feature property showcases, networking opportunities, and special offers for new clients.

Details about the grand opening event will be announced soon. Follow us on our social media channels and subscribe to our newsletter for updates about the celebration and other exciting developments.
