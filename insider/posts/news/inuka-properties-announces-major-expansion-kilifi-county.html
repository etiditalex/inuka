Inuka Properties Announces Major Expansion across Kilifi County

Press Release Inuka Properties
Share:

Inuka Afrika Properties Limited has officially announced its strategic expansion across Kilifi County, marking a significant milestone in the company's growth journey. The expansion includes properties in Mariakani, Mtwapa, Kikambala, Kilifi, Bofa, Tezo, Chumani, Msabaha, and Malindi.

Strategic Expansion Details

This comprehensive expansion represents Inuka Properties' commitment to making real estate investment accessible across the entire coastal region of Kenya. The company will now offer a diverse portfolio of properties spanning nine strategic locations within Kilifi County.

New Locations Include:

  • Mariakani: Strategic location along the Mombasa-Nairobi highway with excellent connectivity
  • Mtwapa: Growing residential and commercial hub with modern amenities
  • Kikambala: Beachfront properties with high tourism potential
  • Kilifi: County headquarters with government and commercial opportunities
  • Bofa: Emerging area with significant development potential
  • Tezo: Agricultural and residential development opportunities
  • Chumani: Strategic location for mixed-use developments
  • Msabaha: Growing residential area with infrastructure development
  • Malindi: Tourism hub with beachfront and inland property options

Investment Opportunities

The expansion brings diverse investment opportunities across different property types:

Residential Properties

From affordable housing units starting at KES 299,000 to luxury beachfront villas, the expansion offers options for every budget and lifestyle preference.

Commercial Properties

Strategic commercial plots and retail spaces in high-traffic areas, perfect for businesses looking to establish a presence in growing markets.

Beach Properties

Exclusive beachfront plots and properties in Kikambala and Malindi, offering unique investment opportunities in Kenya's coastal tourism sector.

Farm Land in Chakama

Agricultural land opportunities in Chakama, perfect for farming ventures and long-term agricultural investments.

Affordable Housing Offplan

Off-plan affordable housing projects across multiple locations, providing flexible payment terms and guaranteed returns on investment.

Company Growth and Vision

This expansion aligns with Inuka Properties' vision of becoming Kenya's leading real estate company, offering comprehensive property solutions across the coastal region.

Key Growth Metrics:

  • Over 147 projects completed since inception
  • More than 4,513 title deeds disbursed to clients
  • 200+ workforce across all locations
  • 9+ years of industry experience

Client Benefits

This expansion brings several benefits to our clients:

Increased Options

Clients now have access to a wider range of properties across different locations, price points, and property types.

Local Expertise

Our team will have local knowledge and presence in each area, ensuring better service and support for our clients.

Flexible Payment Plans

All properties across the new locations will be available with flexible payment terms, making real estate investment accessible to more people.

Comprehensive Services

From property selection to title deed processing, clients will have access to our full range of services in all new locations.

Infrastructure and Development

The expansion takes into account the ongoing infrastructure development in Kilifi County, including:

  • Road network improvements and new highway projects
  • Water and electricity infrastructure upgrades
  • Healthcare and education facility developments
  • Tourism infrastructure enhancements

Future Plans

This expansion is just the beginning of Inuka Properties' growth strategy. The company plans to:

  • Continue expanding to other counties in Kenya
  • Introduce new property types and investment products
  • Enhance digital platforms for better client experience
  • Strengthen partnerships with financial institutions

Community Impact

Beyond business growth, this expansion will have a positive impact on local communities:

  • Job creation in construction, sales, and property management
  • Infrastructure development and community amenities
  • Economic growth through increased property values
  • Improved living standards through affordable housing options

Conclusion

This major expansion across Kilifi County represents a significant step forward for Inuka Properties and our clients. By offering diverse investment opportunities across nine strategic locations, we're making real estate investment more accessible and profitable for everyone.

We invite all current and potential clients to explore these new opportunities and be part of our continued growth story. Together, we can build a brighter future for real estate investment in Kenya.
